About the Program

The PA Army National Guard Child and Youth Services program supports the children and youth of our military families through programming, events, and resources designed to strengthen resilience and well-being.

Services Offered

  • Youth programming and events
  • Support for military-connected children during deployments
  • Connection to community resources for families
  • School liaison support
  • Youth leadership development opportunities
