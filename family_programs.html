Mission

State Family Program provides Family Assistance, promotes Family Readiness, conducts Child & Youth programming, and enriches Family communication skills through outreach and education to support Pennsylvania's Military Families.

8 Essential Services

  • ID Card & DEERS Information
  • TRICARE
  • Financial Readiness
  • Legal
  • Crisis Intervention
  • Emergency Family Assistance Center (EFAC)
  • Exceptional Family Member Program (EFMP)
  • Community Information & Outreach Services

Additional Services

  • Military, Veteran, & Retiree Benefits (All Branches)
  • Deployment Cycle Support & Outreach
  • Educational Programs
  • Employment & Career Services
  • Behavioral & Mental Health Services
  • Household & Property
  • Child & Youth Services & Events
  • Regional Military Share Food Distributions

Volunteer & SFRG Support

  • Hands-on Assistance for Soldier & Family Readiness Groups
  • Orientation & Trainings for Volunteers and Leadership
  • Briefings for Units and Command Teams
  • Connection to Community Resources
  • Information & Referral Assistance

For assistance and additional information, please call your local SFRS listed above.
