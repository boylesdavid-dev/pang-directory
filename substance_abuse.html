About the Program

The PA Army National Guard Substance Abuse Program provides prevention, education, and support services to soldiers and their families dealing with substance abuse issues.

Services Offered

  • Risk Reduction and Prevention Education
  • Suicide Prevention Support
  • Drug Testing Coordination
  • Regional Prevention Coordination
  • Referral to Treatment Services
  • Command Consultation
